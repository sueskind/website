about.me

Hi and welcome to my portfolio site. My name is Jonas and if you went through the sites linked above, you already know on what kind of activities I spend some of my free time. My - let's say - 'artistic' interests are in photography (travel/nature/architecture), graphical design and computer science. I taught those three to myself but chose to study the last one at university, here in Germany.

On my pages you can find a selection of my work. I will update them every now and then. Of course all the content on this site was created by me. The website itself was also made by only me and because I don't have any professional experience in (web-)design, you might encounter one or two design flaws (I also don't own any iOs/Mac devices, so Apple users get the untested version).

Nature and travel show pictures that I took over the years.
On graphic.design you find my work in Photoshop. I got most of my inspiration from YouTube tutorials. (But I promise you, I'm not someone who copies every filter's value exactly to the seventh decimal digit into my own project.)
generative.art is my attempt to show everyone how beautiful algorithms can be. You know, to those 95% that answer "Oh I didn't like that in school." when you tell them you study computer science.
